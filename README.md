# ✨ Gleamworks — Expert Clean, Exceptional Gleam.

A professional, responsive single-page website built for **Gleamworks**, a cleaning business based in the United Kingdom.

---

## 📋 Project Overview

Gleamworks is a UK-based cleaning business offering a wide range of residential and commercial cleaning services. This website was designed and developed to establish a strong online presence, showcase services, and make it easy for potential clients to get in touch.

The site features a modern, polished design with a custom galaxy animation in the hero section, a full services breakdown, gallery of real client photos, transparent pricing information, and direct contact options including phone and WhatsApp.

---

---

## ⭐ Features

- **Hero Section** — Full-screen hero with a custom animated galaxy/shimmer particle effect and clear call-to-action
- **Services Section** — Showcases all 6 cleaning services: Deep Cleans, General Cleans, Appliance Cleans, Start/End of Tenancy, Commercial Property, and HMO Cleans
- **Why Choose Us Section** — Highlights the business values: reliability, affordability, attention to detail, and local personal service
- **Gallery** — Real client photos displayed in a responsive image grid
- **Pricing Section** — Transparent starting price with clear quote process information
- **Contact Section** — Direct phone link and WhatsApp integration for instant client enquiries
- **Tawk.to Live Chat** *(Coming Soon)* — Live chat widget integration planned for future deployment
- **Fully Responsive** — Optimised for mobile, tablet, and desktop screens

---

## 🚀 Deployment

The site is hosted live on **Netlify**, deployed automatically via GitHub integration.

🌐 **Live URL:** [gleamworks.netlify.app](https://gleamworks.netlify.app)

Any push to the `main` branch on GitHub automatically triggers a new deployment on Netlify.
